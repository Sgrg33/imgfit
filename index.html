My Zumba Journey

I discovered Zumba in 2015 and immediately fell in love with how it combines fitness and fun. After experiencing the transformative power of dance fitness in my own life, I became certified to share this joy with others. My classes focus on creating an inclusive environment where everyone can feel comfortable while getting a great workout.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe fitness should be enjoyable and accessible to everyone. My goal is to create a judgment-free zone where you can express yourself through movement while improving your health. Whether you're a beginner or experienced dancer, my classes are designed to challenge and inspire you.
